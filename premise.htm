STARGATE
Premise

Stargates are large rings constructed of a rare mineral which facilitates the discharge of energy in order to form a controlled wormhole.

The Stargate then disassembles objects passing the event horizon, and beams the deconstructed atoms to the destination, where they're reassembled.

Humans discover a ring on earth, and use it to explore the galaxy and meet alien races.
